## 1. Automated Refill Management
**What it is:** Rule-based refill queues reduce manual effort.  
**Why it matters:** Improves adherence, frees staff time, reduces missed revenue.

## 2. AI-Powered Clinical Decision Support
**What it is:** Alerts and guidance based on patient history.  
**Why it matters:** Safer dispensing, better outcomes, stronger payer relationships.

## 3. Integrated Payment & Claims Reconciliation
**What it is:** End-to-end payment + claims visibility.  
**Why it matters:** Less leakage, faster month-end close, cleaner audits.

## 4. Cloud Migration and Remote Access
**What it is:** Secure, role-based web access.  
**Why it matters:** Business continuity, multi-location support, lower IT overhead.

## 5. Advanced Patient Engagement Portals
**What it is:** Refill requests, messaging, reminders.  
**Why it matters:** Higher retention, better experience, more reviews.

## 6. EHR Interoperability Enhancements
**What it is:** FHIR-based data exchange.  
**Why it matters:** Streamlines care coordination with prescribers.

## 7. Predictive Inventory Management
**What it is:** Demand forecasts by SKU + seasonality.  
**Why it matters:** Cuts carrying costs, reduces stockouts and expirations.
